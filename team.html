Our Story

Smooth Press Appliances was founded in 2024 with a vision to revolutionize home ironing solutions. Our founders, a group of engineers and designers in Toronto, identified a need for irons that combined advanced technology with sleek design. They launched the Steam Press series, which quickly gained popularity for its innovative steam technology.

Since then, we’ve grown into a trusted name in home appliances, expanding our product line to include the Smart Press series with AI-powered features. Today, we continue to innovate, ensuring every iron we produce helps our customers press better and look sharper.
