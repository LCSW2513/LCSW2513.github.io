About Smooth Press Appliances

At Smooth Press Appliances, we’re passionate about making ironing effortless and effective. Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Toronto, Canada, our mission is to revolutionize home ironing solutions with cutting-edge technology and sleek design. From our Steam Press series to our AI-powered Smart Press irons, we cater to every ironing need with precision and care.

Our commitment to quality and innovation ensures that every product we create helps you press better and look sharper. Join us in redefining the ironing experience!

Our Story

Smooth Press Appliances was born out of a simple idea: ironing should be easy, efficient, and enjoyable. In 2024, our founders, a group of engineers and designers in Toronto, noticed a gap in the market for high-quality, user-friendly irons. They set out to create a line of irons that combined advanced steam technology with modern design, launching the Steam Press series as our flagship product.

Since then, we’ve expanded our offerings to include the Smart Press series, featuring AI-powered ironing systems and cordless designs. Today, we’re proud to be a trusted name in home appliances, helping customers around the world achieve wrinkle-free perfection.
